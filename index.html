Blindness

Unable to read any Auras, including the default ones of their role and those from Perks. Some essential Auras are exempt from that general rule, usually associated with Killer Power Objects.

Broken

Survivors suffering from Broken are unable to be healed or heal themselves past the Injured State.

Cursed

Survivors suffering from Cursed are currently affected by an active Hex Totem and its effects.

Deep Wound

The health bar progressively drains whenever the Survivor is not running, or in the act of Mending themselves or being Mended by other Survivors. Once the timer runs out, the Survivor is automatically put into the Dying State.

Endurance

Survivors benefitting from Endurance can resist damage from the Killer. If they are hit or otherwise damaged, their character model is briefly outlined in white, which is used to indicate the recent damage-protection through Endurance to the Killer. Endurance is cancelled prematurely whenever the Survivor protected by it performs a Conspicuous Action.

Exhausted

Survivors suffering from Exhaustion are unable to use any of the numerous Exhaustion Perks, preventing them from chaining multiple such Perks in quick succession. Exhausted slowly recovers over time whenever the Survivor is not running. Exhausted recovers immediately after being unhooked.

Exposed

Survivors suffering from Exposed will suffer double-damage when hit by a Basic Attack, which immediately puts them into the Dying State. The Endurance Status Effect can shield a Survivor from double-damage, which would afflict them with Deep Wound instead.

Haemorrhage

Survivors suffering from Haemorrhage produce more Pools of Blood, making them easier to track by the Killer. Survivors suffering from Haemorrhage lose any partial Healing progress at a rate of -7 % per second if not healed up fully.

Haste

Players benefitting from Haste have their Movement speed increased.

Hindered

Players suffering from Hindered have their Movement speed decreased.

Incapacitated

Survivors suffering from Incapacitated are unable to interact with certain Props or other Survivors.

Madness

Survivors suffering from Madness suffer from various hallucinations and other debilitating impairments.

Mangled

Survivors suffering from Mangled take 25 % longer to be healed for 1 Health State. This is achieved by reducing the Healing speed on that Survivor by -20 %. Applies only to healing from the Injured State back to full health.

Oblivious

Survivors suffering from Oblivious are unable to perceive the Killer's Terror Radius and also do not hear the Heartbeat warning of the Killer's proximity. Survivors suffering from Oblivious in turn do not suffer from any effects that rely on Survivors being inside the Terror Radius to be applied.

Sleep Penalty

Asleep Survivors suffering from Sleep Penalty take longer to be woken up from the Dream World by awake Survivors.
